All About Me

My ultimate career goal is to work in game development. I love games and want to be a part of the industry. My short term goal at the moment is to develop my own game in the Unity engine. I want to have the experience of building a game from the ground up and I feel as though it would be a good milestone to reach for me personally. After the game is done, I might go on to make more individual projects if I cannot land a job in the industry outright. I would also like to expand past only being able to use the Unity engine and would explore other options such as the Unreal engine.
